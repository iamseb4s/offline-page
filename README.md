This is the interactive offline/maintenance page for [iamsebas.dev](https://iamsebas.dev). Instead of a boring "down for maintenance" message, users can take command of a spaceship and navigate through a procedurally generated starfield.

## ‚ú® Features

- **Interactive Flight**: Control a spaceship and explore a wormhole-filled universe.
- **Dual Control Schemes**:
  - **Gyroscope**: On supported mobile devices, tilt your phone to steer the ship.
  - **Touch Joystick**: If the gyroscope is unavailable or permission is denied, a virtual joystick appears on-screen for intuitive touch-based control.
- **Desktop Support**: Full control with a mouse on desktop browsers.
- **Dynamic Speed & Braking**: Feel the acceleration and hold your click or touch to brake and steer with more precision.
- **Procedurally Generated Universe**: Every flight is unique, with stars and planets spawned in real-time.
- **Score Persistence**: Your wormhole warp count is saved locally, encouraging you to beat your own high score.
